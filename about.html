✦ About the Index

Arcanium Index is where books become worlds.

Arcanium Index is a curated discovery system for fiction, folklore, audiobooks and world-shaping nonfiction. It is built to make reading feel like exploration again: searchable, visual, social, mythic and easy to enter.

Enter the Index Find a Reading Path
Core idea

We index books that open worlds.

Fiction creates worlds. Nonfiction explains the real one. Folklore connects both.

Fiction

Fantasy, sci-fi, horror, mythic fiction, speculative worlds, epic series and Caribbean-inspired stories where relevant.

Folklore and myth

Caribbean folklore, world mythology, legends, symbols, oral traditions and the mythic patterns behind fantasy.

World-shaping nonfiction

History, geography, environment, psychology, power, politics, technology, climate, creativity and culture.

Design principle

Do not make books feel like homework.

What the Index is becoming

Arcanium Index brings together searchable books, reading paths, rankings, folklore, mythology, audiobooks, selected nonfiction and original writing. It is built for readers who like lore, maps, themes, worldbuilding, hidden gems and stories that leave a mark.
